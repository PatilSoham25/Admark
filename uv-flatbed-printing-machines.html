FAQ UV Flatbed Printer

A UV flatbed printer is used for direct printing on materials like acrylic, glass, wood, metal, ceramic tiles, PVC sheets, leather, and mobile covers for signage, branding, and customized printing applications.

UV flatbed printers provide high-resolution printing, instant UV ink curing, vibrant color output, durable prints, and the ability to print on multiple rigid and flexible materials.

UV flatbed printers are widely used in advertising, interior decoration, signage manufacturing, gift printing, packaging, and industrial printing industries.
