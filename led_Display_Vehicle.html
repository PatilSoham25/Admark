Vehicle LED Display

Maximize your advertising reach with our robust Vehicle LED Displays. Designed specifically for mobility, these screens are perfect for trucks, delivery vans, and taxis. They feature aviation-grade anti-vibration technology, high outdoor brightness, and real-time remote content management via GPS and 4G/WiFi.

Technical Specifications

  • Pixel Pitch: P2.5, P3.0, P4.0, P5.0
  • Brightness: 5,000 - 6,500 cd/m²
  • Power Input: DC 12V / 24V (Vehicle Battery Compatible)
  • Protection Level: IP65 (Waterproof & Dustproof)
  • Lifespan: 100,000 Hours
  • Aviation-grade anti-vibration and shockproof design
  • Smart remote control via 4G, WiFi, and GPS tracking
  • Ultra-slim and lightweight to minimize vehicle load
  • Wide viewing angles to capture street-level audiences
  • Low power consumption preventing battery drain
  • Easy mounting systems tailored for various vehicle types
