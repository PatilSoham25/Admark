Fiber Laser

The Fiber Laser is a powerful CNC laser system specialized for engraving, marking, and cutting metals. It uses fiber optic technology to deliver a concentrated laser beam, enabling deep marking and clean cuts on a wide variety of metals, including stainless steel, aluminum, copper, and brass. Known for its durability and minimal maintenance needs, fiber laser machines are widely used in industries like automotive, electronics, jewelry, and metal fabrication for applications such as barcode marking, serial numbering, and precision part cutting.

Technical Specifications

  • Model:1530S
  • Guide rail : Taiwan SHAC Y axis 25, X, Z axis 20
  • Working area (L*W) : 3000×1500mm
  • Machine gross weight : 1650 KG
  • X/Y axis Repeated Position Precision : ±0.01 mm
  • Voltage : Single phase 220V 50Hz
  • Machine structure : Pipe welding machine body, aviation aluminum beam
  • Transmission system : Dual-drive, precise rack and pinion
  • Auxiliary Parts : Exhaust Fan, Dust and Particle Extraction
