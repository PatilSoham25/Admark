2' x 3' UV Flatbed

The 2' x 3' UV Flatbed Printer is compact and best suited for small-scale printing on rigid materials such as acrylic sheets, MDF, wood, and tiles, making it ideal for customized gift items or signage.

Technical Specifications

  • Usage/Application: Printing
  • Printing Technology: UV Inkjet
  • Ink Type: UV Ink
  • Bed Size: 600 x 900 mm
  • Printing Size/Area: 600 x 900 mm
  • Software: Photo Print
  • Rotary: Attached
  • Speed: 130 sq/ft/hr (On 4 pass)
  • Head: i3200 U1
  • Board: Honson
  • Chiller: Attached
  • Head Option: 3 Available
  • Automatic Material Thickness Measurement for precise and efficient printing
  • Anti-Collision Device - Carriage automatically stops when colliding with obstacles
  • Negative Pressure System - Ensures smooth ink flow and prevents ink leakage
