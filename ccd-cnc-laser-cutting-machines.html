CCD CNC Laser Cutting Machines

The CCD Laser system uses a high-resolution camera (CCD Charge-Coupled Device) to detect registration marks or outlines on pre-printed materials, enabling extremely accurate contour cutting. This makes it perfect for industries working with fabric, printed stickers, heat transfer vinyl, and textile graphics. The automated detection reduces manual alignment efforts and ensures precise cuts every time, especially in workflows involving digital printing followed by laser cutting.
